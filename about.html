Our Mission

Samuelito's Sendoff is dedicated to providing insightful and timely commentary on significant global and domestic events. We aim to cut through the noise and offer clear, concise perspectives on the complex issues shaping our world, and to share a platform for the voices behind the numbers and facts.

About Samuel Ravelo Jr.

Samuel Ravelo Jr. is a dedicated individual with a double major in Political Science and Theatre from Loyola New Orleans. His professional background is diverse, including significant contributions to the non-profit theatre sector as an engineer and consultant for the Entertainment Community Fund. While his work largely keeps him engaged in entertainment and live performance, Samuel remains ever loyal to his family. He firmly believes that even the most humble and committed advocates require a megaphone to disseminate their crucial messages, a conviction that profoundly inspires the ethos of Samuelito's Sendoff.
